## 🌟 The Vision

**Alfonso** isn't just a database; it's the digital pulse of your café. Designed with a "Hardware-First" aesthetic, it bridges the gap between physical stock and digital management. Every interaction is crafted to be as satisfying as pulling a perfect espresso shot.

## 🚀 Key Features

- **⚡ Real-Time Sync:** Powered by Firebase Firestore. When one team member updates a syrup count, the whole kitchen knows instantly.
- **🛡️ Secure Access:** Google Authentication ensures only authorized staff can modify the catalog.
- **📊 Intelligent Stats:** At-a-glance dashboard for Total Catalog, Low Stock alerts, and Out-of-Stock emergencies.
- **🏷️ Smart Categorization:** Organized by Sauces, Syrups, Lazada Supplies, and S&R Supplies for rapid navigation.
- **📱 Mobile-First Design:** A fully responsive, touch-optimized interface that works perfectly on tablets and phones in the heat of service.
- **🎨 Premium Aesthetic:** A custom "Coffee-Dark" theme with glassmorphism and smooth motion transitions.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** [React](https://reactjs.org/) + [Vite](https://vitejs.dev/)
- **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) (Custom Brand Palette)
- **Database:** [Firebase Firestore](https://firebase.google.com/docs/firestore)
- **Auth:** [Firebase Authentication](https://firebase.google.com/docs/auth)
- **Icons:** [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
- **Animations:** [Motion](https://motion.dev/) (motion/react)

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

The interface follows a **"Hardware Specialist"** mood:
- **Color Palette:** Deep coffee blacks, warm tans, and creamy light browns.
- **Typography:** Bold, black-weight headers for maximum scannability.
- **Interactions:** Tactile feedback on buttons and smooth, physics-based modal entries.
